Eminem

  • 1972 Eminem is born
  • 1989 Drops out of college
  • 1995 Birth of daughter
  • 1996 Release of debut album Infinite
  • 1998 Release of 2nd album Slim Shady LP

  • 2002 8 mile movie based on & starring Eminem

  • 2004 Release of 5th album Encore
  • 2005 Release of 6th album Curtain Call
  • 2009 Release of 7th album Relapse
  • 2010 Release of 8th album Recovery
  • 2014 Release of 9th album Marshall Mathers LP 2
  • 2017 Release of 10th album Revival
  • 2018 Release of 11th album Kamikaze
  • 2019 Release of 12th album Music To Be Murdered By
  • 2020 Release of 13th album Music To Be Murdered By B-Side
